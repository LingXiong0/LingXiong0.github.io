This is the first project I did in Typography class. We are asked to combine one letterform and one numeral into a single visual unit. This logotype project was to explore and pay attention to letterforms and typefaces.

This project of space and meaning is to explore the meaning of a word or an idea through spacing, sizing, and placement of letter. As shown in the image, I used the pair, "Powerful" and "Weak". I really enjoy this project because the process of trying to figure out how to show the meaning of the word itself in the simplest way can be difficult yet, interesting.
