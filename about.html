Something About Me

Hello, my name is Ling. I was born and raised in China and around the age of 10, my family moved to the United States. Growing up in two different cultures was a unique experience and due to that, I'm able to become someone who is more open-minded, more empathetic, and willing to accept different voices.

I've always wanted to help people and am willing to explore other interests and ideas. In college, I've taken many different classes related to different fields. I then decided I wanted to learn more about technology, design, and also Human Resource.

Many people thought the combination I had was quite a contrast. However, I believe everything is intertwined. I may not be the person who is centric and focused on one path, but that's okay. I believe having the opportunity to explore different fields allows me to see things from different perspectives.
